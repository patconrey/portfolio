The Experiments

There are two experiment sets. One of them is just testing the ability of people to differentiate between "clean" bonafide and spoofed speech. The other set is testing people's ability to distinguish processed bonafide and spoofed speech.

The experiments are done through Matlab. I'm not super sure how I feel about Matlab. But it's what I'm working with right now. If you don't have Matlab and want to check out the experiments, let me know. I'd be happy to make a Python version. If the scripts don't run, please reach out!

The .zip files have README's in them that describe how to run the experiments. Thanks for checking them out! Click on the links below to download them.
