Learning Publicly

I'm planning to write about things I learn as I learn them. I'm going to do this for a couple reasons: I want to share what I learn and what I'm interested in; Explaining ideas helps me understand them; This gives me an opportunity to make demos.


So, I am going to learn publicly. Whenever I learn something that's interesting, I'll write up an explanation and talk about why I think it's awesome. I don't think this will be made up of exclusively technical content. I'm interested in lots of things outside of engineering: baking, emojis, politics, cats, and corgis.


Also, please note and remember that all my views in this blog are strictly my views. That is, they do not extend to the beliefs of my employers, friends, or families, nor are they representative of anybody's opinions other than my own. They are subject to change without notice. Learning is a process and my opinions, like everything, evolve with time.
